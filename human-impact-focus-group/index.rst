Human impact focus groups
=========================

“Between February and May 2022 and as part of Phase 1 (‘Human Impact’), the
Chair of the Inquiry, Sir Wyn Williams, heard oral evidence from more than 70
affected people in London, Cardiff, Leeds, Glasgow and Belfast. To date, the
Inquiry has gathered information from almost 200 affected Subpostmasters,
including those who have participated in informal Focus Groups.”
