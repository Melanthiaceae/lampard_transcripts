Compensation issues hearings
============================

“Sir Wyn Williams also called hearings on the issue of compensation in July and
December 2022, in recognition that it is a pressing matter for those affected
and in order to provide an opportunity for all parties to inform him of
progress on compensation related issues. He published a `Progress Update
<https://www.postofficehorizoninquiry.org.uk/sites/default/files/2022-08/Progress%20Update%20on%20Issues%20relating%20to%20Compensation%20-%20The%20Post%20Office%20Horizon%20IT%20Inquiry%20-%2015%20August%202022_0.pdf>`_
on Issues relating to Compensation in August 2022 and announced a further
hearing in April 2023 in his `written statement
<https://www.postofficehorizoninquiry.org.uk/news/chair-releases-his-statement-compensation-issues>`_
published 9 January 2023.”
