Phase 2
=======

“Between October and December 2022 and as part of Phase 2 (‘Horizon IT
System’), Sir Wyn Williams heard oral evidence from former government
ministers, Post Office, International Computers Limited, Fujitsu Services
Limited, the National Federation of Subpostmasters and civil servants on the
topics of procurement, design, pilot, roll out and modifications to the Horizon
system. The Inquiry’s technical Expert Witness, Charles Cipione, also gave oral
evidence over two days, 18 October 2022 and 17 November 2022.”
